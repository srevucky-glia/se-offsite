Our Home Loan Options

We pride ourselves on offering competitive rates and terms for a variety of mortgage products. Let's find the one that suits you best:

Fixed-Rate Mortgages

Enjoy the stability of a **fixed interest rate** for the entire loan term, typically 15 or 30 years. Your monthly principal and interest payments remain the same, providing predictable budgeting and peace of mind.

Adjustable-Rate Mortgages (ARMs)

With an ARM, your interest rate starts low for an initial period and then **adjusts periodically** based on market conditions. This can be a good option if you plan to move or refinance before the adjustable period begins.

FHA Loans

Backed by the Federal Housing Administration, FHA loans are ideal for **first-time homebuyers** or those with lower credit scores. They often require a lower down payment and have more flexible credit requirements.

VA Loans

Designed for eligible **veterans, active-duty service members, and their spouses**, VA loans offer significant benefits like no down payment and no private mortgage insurance (PMI). Bank Makebelieve is proud to support our service members.

Jumbo Loans

If you're looking to finance a home that exceeds the conforming loan limits in your area, our **jumbo loan options** can provide the financing you need for higher-value properties. We offer competitive rates and terms for these larger loans.

Refinance Mortgages

Already own a home? Explore our **refinance options** to lower your interest rate, reduce your monthly payments, convert your loan term, or cash out equity for home improvements or debt consolidation.
